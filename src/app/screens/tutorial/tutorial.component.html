How to Play

1. First make a account or log into a existing account .

2. A list of monster cards will be generated. Click on 3 of the cards to create your team..

3. Pick your enemy team, or click random team to have one picked for you.

4. Click Fight to begin Combat.

Combat Rules

1. Choose a monster from your team to fight with and pick a enemy monster to fight.

2. The monsters will automatically roll inititive. If you roll higher and faster you go first.

3. If you are faster you may make attack. If the enemy is faster, they immediatly attack.

4. When it's your turn, you may use a item, or you may make a attack.

5. Items can heal, damage, buff and debuff debuff, but you may only use each item once.

6. If you attack, the monster will roll to hit. If it rolls higher than the enemy Armor class, you deal damage.

7. Your monster will trade blows with the enemy until one of them has their health reduced to 0.

8. Upon hitting 0, the round ends and the dead monster is removed from play .

9. The match continues until you or the enemy has all their monsters slain.

10. Good Luck and have fun.
